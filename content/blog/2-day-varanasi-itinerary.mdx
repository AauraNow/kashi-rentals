# 2-Day Varanasi Itinerary by Bike - Perfect Weekend Trip

Planning a quick weekend trip to Varanasi? This optimized 2-day itinerary on a rented bike/scooty helps you experience the best of Banaras without missing major attractions.

## Why Bike/Scooty is Perfect for This Itinerary

- Cover 40+ km across 2 days efficiently
- Flexible timings for sunrise/sunset
- Easy parking at all locations
- Cost-effective (₹299-499/day vs ₹700+ in autos)
- Freedom to explore spontaneously

## Day 1: Ghats, Temples & Old Varanasi

### 5:30 AM - Assi Ghat Sunrise
**Duration:** 1 hour
Start your day with the famous Assi Ghat sunrise and morning aarti. Park your bike at designated parking (₹10).

**Route:** From hotel → Assi Ghat (easy to find on GPS)

### 7:00 AM - Breakfast at Assi
**Location:** Pizzeria Vaatika Café or Brown Bread Bakery
Try: Lassi, poha, upma

### 8:00 AM - BHU Campus & Vishwanath Temple
**Duration:** 1.5 hours
**Route:** Assi → BHU Main Gate (2 km, 6 mins)

Visit:
- Bharat Kala Bhavan Museum
- New Vishwanath Temple
- BHU Gardens

### 10:00 AM - Tulsi Manas Temple & Durga Temple
**Duration:** 1 hour
**Route:** BHU → Durga Temple (2 km, 7 mins)

Both temples allow bike parking nearby.

### 11:30 AM - Back to Hotel (Rest)
Varanasi is hot during noon. Rest 11:30 AM - 4:00 PM

### 4:00 PM - Explore Banaras Old City
**Route:** Your hotel → Godowlia Chowk

Park bike at paid parking and explore on foot:
- Kashi Vishwanath Temple (darshan)
- Gali Gawakdh (narrow lanes)
- Thatheri Bazaar (brass market)
- Vishwanath Gali shopping

### 6:00 PM - Dashashwamedh Ghat Evening Aarti
**Duration:** 1 hour
**Must-do:** Witness the grand Ganga Aarti

Park bike 500m away and walk to ghat.

### 7:30 PM - Dinner at Godowlia
Try: Kashi Chat Bhandar, Deena Chat Bhandar

### 8:30 PM - Return to Hotel
Safe night riding on main roads.

**Day 1 Total Distance:** ~15 km

## Day 2: Sarnath & Manikarnika Ghat

### 6:00 AM - Manikarnika Ghat
**Duration:** 30 mins
Experience the cremation ghat in early morning (respectful photography only).

### 7:00 AM - Breakfast
Quick breakfast near your area

### 8:00 AM - Sarnath Trip
**Distance:** 10-12 km from Varanasi (25 mins ride)
**Duration at Sarnath:** 3 hours

**What to See:**
- Dhamek Stupa
- Mulagandha Kuti Vihar
- Sarnath Archaeological Museum
- Ashoka Pillar remains
- Thai Temple

**Tips:**
- Entry: ₹20-50 per monument
- Museum closed on Fridays
- Guides available (₹300-500)

### 12:00 PM - Lunch at Sarnath
Local restaurants near the monuments

### 1:30 PM - Return to Varanasi
**Route:** Sarnath → Hotel (rest time)

### 4:00 PM - Ramnagar Fort (Optional)
**Distance:** 8 km from city center
**Duration:** 1.5 hours

Vintage car museum and Ganga views

### 6:00 PM - Assi Ghat Sunset
End your trip where it began - beautiful sunset at Assi

### 7:30 PM - Farewell Dinner
Try: Baati Chokha at local restaurants

**Day 2 Total Distance:** ~35 km

## Safety Tips

1. **Wear Helmet:** Mandatory and provided
2. **Avoid Rush Hours:** 10 AM-12 PM, 5-7 PM
3. **Night Riding:** Stick to main roads
4. **Parking:** Use paid parking only
5. **Fuel:** Don't let tank go below 25%
