## BHU Campus Riding Tips

### 1. Campus Speed Limits
- Main roads: 30 km/hr max
- Hostel areas: 20 km/hr max
- Near Library, Vishwanath Temple: 15 km/hr

### 2. Best Parking Spots

**During Classes:**
- Department parking (free, but arrives early)
- Cycle stands (park near them)
- Avoid hostel gate parking (traffic issue)

**Library/Central Facilities:**
- Dedicated 2-wheeler parking near Central Library
- Sayaji Rao Gaekwad Library parking
- Sports complex parking

### 3. Avoid These Times
- 8:30-9:00 AM (rush hour, classes starting)
- 1:00-1:30 PM (lunch rush)
- 5:00-5:30 PM (class end, hostel mess time)
