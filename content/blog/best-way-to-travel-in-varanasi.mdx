# Best Way to Travel in Varanasi - Complete 2026 Guide

Varanasi, also known as Banaras or Kashi, is one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities. Navigating this ancient city requires understanding your transportation options. This comprehensive guide helps you choose the best way to travel in Varanasi.

## Transportation Options in Varanasi

### 1. Renting a Scooty (Recommended)
**Best for:** Solo travelers, couples, budget tourists

**Advantages:**
- Navigate narrow lanes easily
- Most economical at ₹299/day
- Complete freedom to explore
- Easy parking near ghats and temples
- Fuel efficient (45-50 kmpl)

**Disadvantages:**
- Requires valid driving license
- Not ideal in heavy rain
- Need to be comfortable with traffic

**Perfect for exploring:** Ghats circuit, temple tours, BHU campus, Sarnath day trip

### 2. Renting a Bike
**Best for:** Experienced riders, long-distance travel

**Advantages:**
- More power for highway rides
- Comfortable for two riders
- Better for outstation trips
- Premium riding experience

**Starting from:** ₹499/day for Honda CB Shine, ₹799/day for Royal Enfield

[Rent a Bike in Varanasi](/bike-rental-in-varanasi)

### 3. Auto Rickshaws
**Best for:** Short distances, no license

**Typical Fares:**
- Varanasi Cantt to Godowlia: ₹80-100
- Assi to Dashashwamedh: ₹50-70
- BHU to Cantt: ₹100-120

**Disadvantages:**
- Meter rarely used
- Bargaining required
- Can't reach narrow lanes
- Costs add up quickly

### 4. Cycle Rickshaws
**Best for:** Old city exploration, short distances

**Advantages:**
- Environment friendly
- Access to narrower lanes
- Cultural experience
- Budget friendly (₹20-50)

**Disadvantages:**
- Very slow
- Limited distance coverage
- Not comfortable in heat

### 5. App Cabs (Ola/Uber)
**Availability:** Limited in Varanasi
**Best for:** Airport/railway station transfers

**Typical Fares:**
- Airport to Assi: ₹350-450
- Cantt to Assi: ₹150-200

### 6. Walking
**Best for:** Ghat exploration, old city lanes

Many areas of old Varanasi are best explored on foot, especially the ghats and temple lanes.

## Tips for Riding in Varanasi

1. **Start Early**: Less traffic before 9 AM
2. **Helmet Mandatory**: Always wear helmet (provided with rental)
3. **GPS Navigation**: Download offline maps
4. **Fuel Stations**: Available in Sigra, BHU, Cantt areas
5. **Parking**: Use paid parking near ghats (₹10-20)
6. **Traffic**: Left-hand traffic, be cautious at roundabouts

## Best Areas to Stay for Easy Transportation

1. **Assi Ghat**: Best for tourists, close to everything
2. **Sigra**: Central location, well-connected
3. **Lanka/BHU**: Student area, budget friendly
4. **Mahmoorganj**: Near Cantt railway station
